Miss Mary's Closet

Miss Mary's Closet, located on the BFCS campus at 1522 East Southern Avenue in Phoenix, provides free and gently used clothing for children from newborn 18 years of age. Any parent with a child enrolled in a BFCS program may complete a request for clothing.

Cash donations are preferred to adequately keep clothing inventory current .

Children may choose two outfits from the clothing bank inventory when they qualify, based on their parents' income level.

Any parent with a child enrolled in a BFCS program may apply.

Miss Mary's Closet is open from 9 a.m. to 12 noon, Tuesdays and Fridays during the school year.

Donations of new clothing items are always welcome. To make a donation, call (602) 243-1773.
