Family Support and Preservation Program

The Family support Program is a family centered, community based program that provides a continuum of services for the purposes of strengthening and improving family functioning so that the needs of family members can be met and unnecessary out-of-home placements can be prevented, while ensuring the well-being and safety of children and all other family members.

The Family Support Program offers a continuum of services, such as direct provision or referral for basic needs. The following services are available:

• Help families identify strengths and resources
• Assist in locating emergency shelter
• Provide emergency food, diapers and clothing
• Provide information about positive parenting
• Assist in obtaining child day care
• Help enroll in GED preparation programs
• Assist families in connecting with community resources
• Provide family members with supportive counseling
• Help parents and individuals find jobs

Please call (602) 243-1773, Monday through Friday between 8 a.m.
and 5 p.m. and ask for the Family Preservation/Family Support Program.

Our culturally diverse staff provides services in your home or office and in the community – wherever it is most convenient for you.
